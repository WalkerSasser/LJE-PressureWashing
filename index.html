About

Find Out More About Us

New local business to provide quality pressure washing service.

LJE Pressure Washing is a new local business with the sky as its limits. Our services include pressure washing home exteriors, concrete, brick, stone, pool decks, fencing, wood decks, and house washes up to two stories.

We offer free estimates and are available at any time. We want the best for our customers and are committed to quality work and customer satisfaction. As we continue to grow and improve our work, we want to continue to serve our community. See for yourself and have your exterior cleaned today!
